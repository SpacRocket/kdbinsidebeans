Union of two lists

x union y    union[x;y]

Where x and y are lists or atoms, returns a list of the distinct items of its combined arguments, i.e. distinct x,y.

q)1 2 3 3 6 union 2 4 6 8
1 2 3 6 4 8
q)distinct 1 2 3 3 6, 2 4 6 8      / same as distinct on join
1 2 3 6 4 8
