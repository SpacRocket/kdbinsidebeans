Shift the items of a list to the left or right

x rotate y    rotate[x;y]

Where

  • x is an integer atom
  • y is a list

returns y rotated by x items. Rotation is to the ‘left’ for positive x, to the ‘right’ for negative x.

q)2 rotate 2 3 5 7 11    / rotate a list
5 7 11 2 3
q)-2 rotate 2 3 5 7 11
7 11 2 3 5
